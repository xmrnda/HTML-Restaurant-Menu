Origin Story

On June 13, 2013, Kim Namjoon opened H-Thai-ML on Namkanda, Thailand. After one year of wild success, increasing popularity, and numerous culinary awards, he opened his second H-Thai-ML on BTS Island with his 6 brothers. The rest is history, and the best is Yet To Come.
